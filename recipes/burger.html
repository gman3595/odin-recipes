Ingredients

  • 2lbs ground chuck
  • 1/2 cup crushed saltines
  • 1 large egg
  • 2tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 2tbsp milk
  • salt & pepper to taste
  • 1tsp garlic powder
  • 1tsp onion powder

Directions

  1. Add ground chuck, crushed crackers, egg, Worcestershire, milk, salt and pepper, garlic powder, and onion powder to a large bowl and mix by hand until the mixture is very smooth
  2. Press the meat down in a bowl into an even disk. Use knife to cut and divide the hamburger patty mixture into 6 1/3lb grill or skillet patties or 12 thin griddle patties
  3. Set out a baking sheet lined with wax paper or foil to hold the patties. Use a spoon to press a dent in the center of each patty so they don't puff up as they cook.
  4. Preheat the grill or skillet to medium heat
  5. For thick patties: grill or fry the patties for 3-4 mins per side / thin patties: 2 mins per side
  6. Stack the hot patties on hamburger buns and top with your favorite hamburger toppings
